H & B Legal and Consultant is a full-service boutique law firm comprising a team of experienced and energetic legal professionals providing high quality and multi-faceted legal services across a spectrum of complex legal issues. We have substantive legal expertise in various disciplines, sectors and industries with an in-depth understanding of administrative, regulatory and legislative processes with a single point solution. As a growing firm, we are staunch believers in going beyond the call of duty. The firm consists of a young, passionate and driven team of lawyers mentored by wellestablished leaders in its domain practice areas.

The firm’s expertise coupled with effective, timely and practical solutions is the factors that are predominant in choosing the firm by the clients. The firm’s vision is to provide continued excellent solutions to the clients as a full-service law firm. The firm keeps its client regularly informed about legal development.

The firm handles matters before District Courts, Judicial and Quasi-Judicial Forums, Tribunals, Commissions, High Courts and the Supreme Court of India
